Short-form Reel
A 15–60 second vertical video for Instagram Reels, TikTok, or YouTube Shorts. AI-scripted, voiced, captioned, and sound-designed using Higgsfield's cinematic engine and HeyGen avatars. No filming required.
02
Branded Still Post
A static image for your feed — spotlights, quotes, offers, announcements. Designed to your brand colors and fonts so your grid looks consistent and intentional, not random.
03
Ad Creative Set
3–5 ready-to-run paid ad visuals for Meta or TikTok Ads Manager. Multiple sizes (1:1, 4:5, 9:16), headline copy, hook variants, and CTA overlays — the fuel for your paid campaigns.
04
Brand Color Kit
Your primary, secondary, and accent colors as hex codes with usage rules. Applied to every reel, post, and ad so your brand is instantly recognisable mid-scroll.
05
Brand Identity Document
A full PDF: logo rules, color palette, typography, tone of voice, and visual do's and don'ts. Your creative bible — shared with any collaborator to maintain consistency.
06
Content Bible
Full content strategy: monthly pillars, posting schedule, audience personas, platform tone, and a 90-day roadmap. Your north star for everything published.
07
Performance Report
A clear breakdown of reach, impressions, engagement rate, follower growth, and top posts — with actionable recommendations on what to scale next month.
08
Revision Round
One complete pass of your feedback applied across all deliverables. You share notes, we implement. Authority clients get 5 full rounds, unlimited on final outputs.
09
HeyGen Avatar
A photorealistic AI spokesperson — stock or a custom clone of you — that presents to camera in your reels. No filming, no studio, no scheduling headaches.
10
A/B Copy Variants
Two or more versions of the same ad with different hooks, headlines, or CTAs. Run split tests, find what converts, then scale the winner with confidence.
